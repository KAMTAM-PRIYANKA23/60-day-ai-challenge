# 📦 Interactive Supply Chain Builder

An interactive simulation that lets users design an end-to-end supply chain by making strategic business decisions across every stage of the logistics network.

Built as part of my **#60DaysOfClaudeAI Challenge (Day 30)**, this project transforms traditional supply chain concepts into an engaging, decision-based learning experience where every choice impacts business performance.

---

## 🚀 Features

* 📦 Build a complete supply chain from scratch
* 🏭 Choose manufacturing strategies
* 🚚 Select transportation methods
* 🏬 Configure warehouse operations
* 📊 Manage inventory policies
* 📈 Live business performance metrics
* ⚖️ Cost vs Speed vs Sustainability trade-offs
* 🎯 Interactive decision-based simulation
* 💻 Responsive single-page application
* ⚡ Built using pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript

---

## 🎮 How It Works

The simulation guides users through the five major stages of supply chain design:

### 1. Supplier Selection

Choose suppliers based on cost, reliability, and sustainability.

### 2. Factory Strategy

Select the most suitable manufacturing location while balancing operational efficiency and expenses.

### 3. Warehouse Planning

Determine how products are stored and distributed to customers.

### 4. Transportation

Compare different logistics options such as road, rail, and sea freight to optimize delivery speed and operational costs.

### 5. Inventory Management

Choose an inventory strategy that balances product availability, storage costs, and business risk.

Every decision instantly updates key business metrics, allowing users to observe how strategic choices influence overall supply chain performance.

These metrics help visualize the trade-offs involved in designing an efficient supply chain.
