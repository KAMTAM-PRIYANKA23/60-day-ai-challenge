## Objective

## Topic Covered

### Role-Based Prompting

Role-Based Prompting is a technique where we assign a role or persona to the AI before asking a question. This helps the AI respond from a specific perspective, expertise level, or professional background.

Example:

* Without Role: "How do I launch a product?"
* With Role: "You are a Product Manager. How do I launch a product?"

The second prompt provides more focused and expert-level guidance.

## Key Learnings

* Assigning a role gives AI additional context.
* Role-Based Prompting improves response quality and relevance.
* Different roles produce different perspectives for the same question.
* AI can simulate expertise from various domains when provided with a clear role.
* Better prompts lead to more useful and actionable outputs.
* The clearer the role, the better the response.

## Example Comparison

### Without Role Prompt

Prompt:
How do I improve my resume?

Output:
General resume tips and suggestions.

### With Role Prompt

Prompt:
You are an experienced Technical Recruiter. How do I improve my resume for software engineering internships?

Output:
Specific recruiter-focused advice, industry expectations, and practical improvements.

## Benefits of Role-Based Prompting

1. More accurate responses
2. Better context and relevance
3. Professional and actionable insights
