# 🚛 AI Supply Chain Control Tower

An interactive, real-time **Supply Chain Operations Simulator** where you take on the role of the **Head of Operations** and make strategic decisions to keep a global logistics network running efficiently.

Designed as a premium dark-themed control center, this simulation combines operational strategy, business KPIs, and time-based decision making into an engaging management experience.

---

## 🌟 Overview

Supply chain leaders constantly face disruptions that require immediate action.

This project simulates those real-world scenarios by generating dynamic operational alerts that impact key business metrics. Every decision influences operational performance, customer satisfaction, inventory health, transportation efficiency, operating costs, and overall business success.

Can you keep the supply chain running before time runs out?

## 🎮 Gameplay

Players must evaluate each operational alert and choose the most effective response.

Available actions include:

* Expedite Shipment
* Use Backup Supplier
* Reroute Trucks
* Increase Production
* Transfer Inventory
* Approve Air Freight
* Delay Decision
* Ignore Alert

Every decision has immediate and sometimes delayed consequences, making strategic prioritization essential.
