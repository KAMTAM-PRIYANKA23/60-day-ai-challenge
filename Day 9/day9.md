# Day 9 – NutriScope 🍎📊

## Overview

NutriScope is a Nutrition Intelligence Dashboard built as part of the AI Product Building Challenge.

The application helps users track their daily food intake, analyze nutritional consumption, identify deficiencies, and receive personalized recommendations based on dietary preferences and nutritional goals.

This project was developed using AI-assisted development and enhanced through iterative prompting techniques.

---

## Objective

To build a modern nutrition analytics platform that transforms food logs into actionable health insights through data visualization and recommendation systems.
