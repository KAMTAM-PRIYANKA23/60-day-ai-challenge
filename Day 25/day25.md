# 🦈 AI Shark Tank Simulator

> **Pitch your startup. Face AI investors. Get funded—or learn why not.**

An interactive **AI Shark Tank Simulator** built as a **single self-contained HTML application** that recreates the excitement of pitching a startup to investors. Enter your business idea, answer challenging investor questions, receive AI-powered evaluations, and download a professional investment report—all without requiring a backend.

---

# 🚀 Demo

Experience a realistic startup funding simulation where four AI investors analyze your business from different perspectives and make an investment decision based on your responses.

---

# ✨ Features

## 📝 Startup Idea Submission

Provide your startup information including:

* Startup Name
* Problem Statement
* Solution
* Revenue Model
* Target Audience
* Funding Ask

---

## 🦈 AI Shark Panel

Meet four unique AI investors:

### Venture Capitalist

* Focuses on market size
* Scalability
* Growth potential

### Founder

* Evaluates execution
* Product vision
* Team capability

### Customer

* Measures usability
* Customer value
* Real-world adoption

### Angel Investor

* Reviews profitability
* Revenue potential
* Return on investment

---

## 🎤 Interactive Pitch Session

* AI-generated startup pitch
* Investors ask role-specific questions
* Respond to investor challenges
* Dynamic AI reactions

---

## 📊 Startup Scoring

Each startup receives scores based on:

* Market Potential
* Innovation
* Business Model
* Execution
* Investment Worthiness

Overall Score:
**0–100**

---

## 💰 Investment Decision

The AI panel recommends one of the following:

* ✅ Invest
* ❌ Reject
* 🤝 Acquire
* ⏳ Come Back Later

Also generates:

* Suggested Valuation
* Recommended Funding Amount
* Detailed Investment Reasoning

---

## 📄 Professional Pitch Report

Generate a downloadable PDF containing:

* Startup Summary
* Investor Questions
* Your Responses
* Detailed Scores
* Investment Decision
* Valuation
* Funding Recommendation
* Strengths
* Weaknesses
* Suggestions for Improvement
