# 🚨 Operation Lifeline – AI Supply Chain Crisis Simulator

> *Navigate uncertainty. Make critical decisions. Build a resilient future.*

## 📖 Overview

**Operation Lifeline** is an interactive AI-powered Supply Chain Crisis Simulator that places users in the role of executive leadership during a real-world supply chain disruption.

Rather than presenting theory, the simulator creates an immersive decision-making experience where every choice carries consequences. Users must balance cost, customer satisfaction, operational continuity, supplier relationships, and long-term resilience while responding to an unfolding crisis.

The project demonstrates how strategic thinking and AI-driven planning can transform supply chain management from reactive problem-solving into proactive resilience.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The goal of this simulator is to help users understand:

* How supply chain disruptions impact businesses
* The trade-offs behind executive decision-making
* The importance of supplier relationships
* Crisis communication and leadership under pressure
* The role of Artificial Intelligence in building resilient supply chains

---

# ✨ Key Features

### 🏢 Company Briefing

Start by analyzing a company's operational profile, including revenue, inventory, factories, warehouses, suppliers, and lead times.

### ⚠️ Crisis Response War Room

Respond to a live supply chain disruption by selecting strategic actions that balance speed, cost, inventory, and customer satisfaction.

### 🤝 Supplier Negotiation

Participate in a multi-round negotiation where every decision influences supplier trust, pricing, and delivery priority.

### 👔 CEO Boardroom

Step into the role of CEO and make executive-level decisions involving media communication, financial planning, customer commitments, and organizational leadership.

### 🤖 AI Strategy Planning

Choose AI investments that strengthen long-term resilience through forecasting, supplier monitoring, inventory optimization, and intelligent procurement.

### 📊 Performance Report

Receive a comprehensive evaluation of your crisis management strategy across multiple business dimensions, including:

* Leadership
* Negotiation
* Cost Control
* Customer Satisfaction
* Risk Management
* Organizational Resilience

Along with personalized insights, key takeaways, and recommendations for future improvement.

---

# 💡 What Makes This Project Unique?

Unlike traditional educational tools, Operation Lifeline focuses on **decision-making under uncertainty**.

There are no universally correct answers.

Every decision introduces new trade-offs, forcing users to think like real executives responsible for balancing financial performance, operational stability, customer trust, and long-term growth.

The simulator transforms complex business concepts into an engaging, interactive learning experience.
