The E85 Paradox — Verdict
E85 SCORE 5.7/10
⚠ Counterintuitive

E85 pours 18% cheaper at the pump (₹82 vs ₹100/L), but the Brezza's flex‑fuel mileage drop is steep enough that real‑world running cost ends up 2.6% higher per km than ordinary Petrol — the classic "cheap fuel, expensive habit" trap. Pump price would need to fall to ₹79.10/L just to break even. Where E85 wins outright is emissions: 45% less CO₂ per km than Petrol, the best of any fuel tested.
