Privacy Improvement Plan
Actionable Steps to Improve Your Privacy Score VERIFIED ADVICE
01
Audit TikTok & Instagram App Permissions
Go to your phone's Settings → Apps → TikTok / Instagram → Permissions. Revoke Camera, Microphone, Location, and Contacts access if not actively needed. These apps do not require all permissions to function.
+12 Privacy Score· High Impact· 5 min
02
Turn Off Google Ad Personalisation
Visit myaccount.google.com → Data & Privacy → Ad Settings → Turn off personalised ads. This limits Google's ability to build a cross-app advertising profile linking Search, YouTube, and Maps.
+9 Privacy Score· High Impact· 3 min
03
Enable End-to-End Encryption for WhatsApp Chat Backups
WhatsApp → Settings → Chats → Chat Backup → End-to-end Encrypted Backup → Turn On. Without this, your chat backups stored on Google Drive or iCloud are not encrypted by default.
+7 Privacy Score· Medium Impact· 2 min
04
Use a Privacy-First Search Engine
Replace Google Search with DuckDuckGo or Brave Search for general browsing. Google Search is your single highest-exposure data point — reducing its use meaningfully limits your estimated behavioural profile.
+10 Privacy Score· High Impact· 10 min setup
05
Review Snapchat & TikTok Location Sharing
Snapchat's Snap Map and TikTok's location services can expose real-time or estimated location data. Set Snap Map to "Ghost Mode" and restrict TikTok location to "Never" in iOS/Android settings.
+6 Privacy Score· Medium Impact· 4 min
06
Use a Separate Email for Shopping Apps
Create a dedicated email for Amazon and Meesho purchases. This limits cross-service data linkage and reduces the ability of data brokers to correlate your shopping identity with your social profiles.
+5 Privacy Score· Medium Impact· 15 min
