# 🧠 Cognitive Pattern Explorer

**Day 36 of #60DaysOfAI**

**Cognitive Pattern Explorer** is an interactive, psychology-inspired self-reflection experience that helps users explore their thinking tendencies through guided scenarios, prioritization exercises, and visual mapping activities.

Designed as a **single-file HTML application**, the experience encourages curiosity and self-awareness without evaluating or diagnosing mental health. Every interaction is educational, reflective, and designed to promote deeper understanding of personal decision-making patterns.

> **Disclaimer:** This application is for educational and self-reflection purposes only. It does **not** diagnose, assess, or provide medical or psychological advice.

---

# 🌟 Features

## 🌿 Calm Start Experience

Begin by selecting a preferred reflection mode:

* 🌸 Calm Mode
* ⚡ Stress Mode

The selected mode personalizes the experience while maintaining a supportive and exploratory tone.

---

# 📖 Chapter 1 — Discover Your Thinking Style

Explore realistic everyday scenarios that encourage reflection rather than testing.

Possible thinking tendencies include:

* 🔍 Analytical Thinker
* ❤️ Emotional Intuitive
* 🔄 Overthinking Loop Style
* 🚀 Action-First Decision Maker
* ⚖️ Balanced Reflective Thinker

Instead of assigning labels, the application explains how different choices may reflect common thinking preferences.

---

# 🧩 Chapter 2 — Choose Your Priorities

An interactive drag-and-drop activity where users organize priorities based on how they naturally approach decisions.

Examples include:

* Logic
* Emotions
* Speed
* Planning
* Relationships
* Creativity
* Evidence
* Experience

This chapter demonstrates how priorities influence decision-making.

---

# 🗺️ Chapter 3 — Map Your Thinking

Visualize personal thought processes using an interactive draggable timeline.

Arrange different stages such as:

* Observe
* Analyze
* Feel
* Compare
* Decide
* Reflect
* Act

The application explains how different sequences represent different thinking approaches.

---

# 📔 Final Reflection Journal

Complete the experience with a personalized reflection dashboard featuring:

* 🌟 Thinking tendency percentage breakdown
* 💡 Personalized reflective insights
* 📝 Gentle observations about decision-making patterns
* 🌱 Suggestions for balanced thinking
* 📖 Reflection summary

Language remains supportive and educational throughout the experience.

---

# 🎨 User Experience

Designed with a calming modern aesthetic featuring:

* Soft gradients
* Glassmorphism-inspired cards
* Ambient background animations
* Smooth transitions
* Progress indicators
* Responsive layouts
* Keyboard accessibility
* Reduced motion support
* Touch-friendly interactions
* Reusable drag-and-drop components

The experience feels more like a guided journey than a traditional assessment.

# 🛠️ Technology Stack

Built entirely using:

No:

* React
* Frameworks
* npm
* APIs
* Databases

The application is designed to help users:
