## Smart Interview Questions

1. What projects will interns contribute to during the internship?

2. How does the company support intern mentorship and learning?

3. What technologies are used most frequently in day-to-day development?

4. How is work-life balance maintained during project deadlines?

5. Are there opportunities for internship extension or Pre-Placement Offers (PPOs)?

---

## Key Learnings

* Analyze opportunities beyond salary and company reputation.
* Carefully review job requirements and responsibilities.
* Identify hidden risks before applying.
* Research company culture and growth trends.
* Ask targeted interview questions to validate expectations.
* AI tools can simplify career research and decision-making.

This activity helped me understand how to evaluate job opportunities critically using AI. Instead of applying blindly, I learned to analyze job descriptions, identify potential red flags, understand market expectations, and make informed career decisions.

Starting career preparation from the first year provides a long-term advantage and helps build confidence for future internships and placements.
