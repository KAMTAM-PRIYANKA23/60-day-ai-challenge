- Promoter holdings
- Institutional holdings
- Foreign investor participation
- Insider buying or selling trends

5. Evaluate risks:
- Industry risks
- Regulatory risks
- Competitive threats
- Debt concerns
- Governance issues

- Strengths
- Weaknesses

- Threats
