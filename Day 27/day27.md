Hospital Admission Readiness Simulator
📌 Overview

The Hospital Admission Readiness Simulator is an interactive healthcare simulation that allows users to step into the role of a Hospital Admission Coordinator and evaluate whether a patient is ready for hospital admission. The simulator recreates real-world admission workflows by combining administrative tasks, prior authorization, clinical documentation, insurance verification, and risk assessment into a single, engaging experience.

Built as a single-page application, it provides an educational view of how multiple healthcare teams coordinate to ensure safe and compliant patient admissions.
