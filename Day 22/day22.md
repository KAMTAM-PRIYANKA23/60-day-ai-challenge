# Day 22 – Startup Validation Report

# LifeLink

## Emergency Blood & Organ Donor Network

### One-Line Pitch

LifeLink is an AI-powered emergency donor coordination platform that connects patients, hospitals, blood banks, and verified donors in real time to reduce the time required to find life-saving blood and organ donors.

---

# Executive Summary

Healthcare emergencies often require immediate access to blood, platelets, plasma, or organ donors. Unfortunately, families are frequently forced to rely on social media posts, personal contacts, or manual searches during critical situations.

LifeLink aims to solve this problem by creating a centralized emergency donor network that instantly notifies compatible nearby donors when verified requests are created.

The long-term vision is to become the trusted emergency donation infrastructure connecting healthcare providers, donors, and patients.
