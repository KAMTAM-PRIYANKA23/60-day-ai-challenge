# 🌍 Day 8 – Environmental Health Analyzer

## 📌 Project Overview

The Environmental Health Analyzer is an interactive dashboard designed to visualize and analyze environmental health indicators across multiple cities. The application provides insights into air quality, pollution levels, water quality, and overall environmental health scores through dynamic charts and interactive filters.

This project demonstrates how data visualization can be used to understand environmental conditions and support informed decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze environmental health metrics across different cities.
* Compare Air Quality Index (AQI) levels.
* Visualize PM2.5 and PM10 pollution levels.
* Evaluate water quality scores.
* Generate environmental health insights.
* Provide recommendations based on environmental conditions.
